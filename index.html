About True Axis Advisory

True Axis Advisory was founded by Erik Sorensen, a senior federal leader with more than two decades of experience operating in complex, high-consequence environments. His advisory work is grounded in real-world leadership, decision-making, organizational alignment, and execution under pressure.

The firm works with leaders and organizations facing performance breakdowns, unclear ownership, competing priorities, and inconsistent execution. The focus is not theory. The focus is helping leaders define the work, clarify the challenge, align the organization, and improve execution.
